Overview

Axolotl Effect

Axolotl Effect is a 3D puzzle platformer through time. You play as Adhoby, a loyal citizen determined to revive his dying civilisation of axolotls.

Hopes and Dreams Engine

The custom game engine powering Axolotl Effect, written in C++ with custom-built rendering, scripting, and entity component systems (ECS).
